Book a Computer

Please read the guidelines below to book a computer:

  • To reserve a computer, you must have a valid Greater Sudbury Public Library card and a PIN. If you do not have a PIN, you may obtain one by calling or visiting any location of the Greater Sudbury Public Library. If you are a visitor to Greater Sudbury and would like to access the Internet or a word processing station, Visitors cards are available (with proper identification) from Library staff.
  • You may reserve a computer for a maximum of two hours per day, to ensure that more customers have access to the computers.
